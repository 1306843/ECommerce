About optiSave

optiSave is a Charlottesville-based coupon subscription for UVa students, customized based on the interests of our users. Drawn from the experiences of two current Hoos, our newsletter is a tried and true method of saving for everyone who wants the benefits of saving money without the drawbacks of wasting time finding the best way to save.

The Saving Effect

As college students, we know how important saving money is on a daily basis. Whether it's grocery shopping, or buying school supplies, we're here to help . Our efforts are entirely focused on students, from the minds of students themselves.

Every week, we scour every coupon book and online saving site, so that you don't have to. Rather than wasting valuable time (trust us, we know how busy college can get) trying to find a random coupon online, you can simply consult our weekly newsletter.

Saving you time and money. It's that easy.

Each newsletter contains coupons for products and services customized to your lifestyle. Our coupons update in real time, allowing you to utilize the right coupons at the right time. Don't worry about putting every new coupon you find in your wallet and forgetting to use it.
